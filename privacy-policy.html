Who We Are

Savyn is an Android launcher developed by Savyn Labs that transforms your phone into a personalized second brain ‚Äî entirely on-device using on-device AI.

What Data Savyn Accesses

To power its intelligence, Savyn accesses the following on your device only:

üì± Installed Apps

To display your app drawer, create shortcuts, and understand your app ecosystem.

üìä App Usage Patterns

üìù Your Saves

How It's Used

  • Smart Predictions: Show the right apps, notes, and actions when you need them
  • Pattern Recognition: Learn your routines to reduce friction in your day
  • Contextual Retrieval: Surface saved content when it's relevant
  • On-Device ML Only: All intelligence runs locally via TensorFlow Lite

What We Don't Do

  • No server uploads: Your data never leaves your phone
  • No third-party sharing: We don't share data with anyone
  • No selling data: We have nothing to sell ‚Äî we don't have your data
  • No cloud training: Our models don't learn from your usage
  • No advertising: No ads, no tracking pixels, no analytics SDKs

What We Don't Access

Savyn does not access, collect, or process:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Photos, videos, or files (except what you explicitly save)
  • Contacts or calendar events
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Browsing history or app content

Storage & Security

  • Local encrypted database: All data stored in Android's secure app storage
  • No cloud backup by us: If you use system backups, your OS vendor's policies apply
  • Sandboxed execution: Other apps cannot access Savyn's data

Permissions We Request

Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)

This sensitive permission lets Savyn see app usage events. Android protects this permission specially ‚Äî you must explicitly grant it in Settings. We use it solely for on-device learning and never transmit this data.

Your Control

  • Delete anytime: Uninstalling Savyn removes all local data instantly
  • Clear learning: Reset Savyn's learned patterns from within the app
  • No account required: Full functionality without signing up for anything

Children's Privacy

Savyn is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly process data from children. Since we don't collect any personal data, this concern is largely moot.
