How it works

Everything happens through conversation.

No menus. No settings screens. Just talk.

They learn by listening The more you talk, the better they understand. They remember everything.
They check in when it matters Not random notifications. They know when to show up — and when to leave you alone.
Each has their own apps Fitness knows your gym app. Chill knows Netflix. Quick launch from home.
Privacy-first

Your conversations never touch our servers.

  • Local only — All data lives on your device. We cannot see it.
  • Anonymized AI — Personal details are stripped before any AI processing.
  • Zero tracking — No accounts. No ads. No selling your data.
  • Total control — Delete the app, everything goes with it.
Over time, they notice patterns, remember context, and coordinate to help you be who you actually want to be.
