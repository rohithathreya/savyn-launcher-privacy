Introducing

Savyn

Your phone as a second brain

Savyn turns your Android phone into a private, on-device second brain that actually understands you.

Not a separate "system" you have to remember to maintain.

Not another inbox.

Just your default phone experience… made smarter.

here's the thing

Your phone already sees almost everything about your day:

  • ◐ When you wake up and when you crash
  • ◑ Which apps you open, and in what order
  • ◒ Your hobbies and interests
  • ◓ The restaurants you check, the books you look up, the articles you save (and forget)

Right now, all of that lives as scattered, dumb data across different apps and a static app grid.

Savyn's job is simple: make sense of that data for you and put an intelligent, personalised layer on top of your phone.

what it does

What Savyn does

01

Turns your home screen into a contextual "second brain", not just an icon grid

02

Learns your patterns over time – what you use, when, and in what combinations

03

Surfaces the right apps, actions and routines when you're most likely to need them

04

Lets you add what matters – notes, links, lists, ideas – into the same brain

05

Keeps everything private and on-device by design

The more you use your phone, the more Savyn quietly adapts to you.

why on-device?

Why on-device?

A real second brain needs real data about you.

But that doesn't mean shipping your life off to random servers.

Savyn runs its intelligence on your device, so:

  • Your patterns stay on your phone
  • No account is required to get value
  • You stay in control of what you share

Your phone already knows a lot about you.
Savyn's goal is to make it finally useful with that knowledge.
