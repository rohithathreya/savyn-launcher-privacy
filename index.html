The problem

You use 30 apps a day.
None of them talk to each other.

Your fitness app doesn't know about your work deadlines. Your notes app doesn't know what you're interested in. Your phone sees fragments of your life — steps, playlists, messages, orders — but never the full picture.

The idea

What if your phone actually
understood you?

Not a generic assistant that answers questions. A dedicated layer that learns your interests, remembers your conversations, notices your patterns, and surfaces the right thing at the right time. Built around you — not around apps.

How it works

Everything happens through
conversation.

01

Just talk

No menus. No settings screens. Tell Savyn what's on your mind, what you're working on, what you want to remember. It learns from every conversation.

02

It builds around you

Savyn discovers your interests, notices your patterns, and organizes your world. Your home screen adapts. Your apps are surfaced when relevant. Content appears when it matters.

03

Voice-first

Hold the Nebula button and speak. Release toward Daily Ops, Your Mind, or Save as Note. No typing needed — Savyn transcribes in real time.

04

Private by design

All data stays on your phone. AI calls are anonymized through our proxy. No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Uninstall and everything is gone.

The pledge

Your data stays
on your phone.

100% on-device storage.

Conversations, notes, interests, schedule — everything lives locally. We don't have servers that store your data.

Anonymized AI calls.

When AI processing is needed, personal details are stripped before anything leaves your device. AI providers don't train on your data.

No ads. No tracking.

We don't sell data. We don't profile you. We don't show ads. We don't even run analytics SDKs.

Uninstall and it's gone.

No trace. No residue. No cloud backup we control. Your data is yours alone.
